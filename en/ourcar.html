1. Download the Uber app

To start working as a partner driver with Uber, you must Download the Uber app from the official store on your mobile device. Application available for download on iOS and Android platforms.

2. Registration and login to your account

After installing the app, you need to create an Uber driver account or log in to an existing account. When registering, you will need documents and information about your identification, including driver's license, insurance and other necessary documents.

3. Machine feed

After successful registration in The Uber app, you must indicate the availability of your car to accept orders. Important prepare your car for work, making sure it is serviceable and clean.

4. Accepting orders

As soon as your car available for orders, you will receive notifications about new orders. You can consider detailed information about each order, including destination, passenger rating and estimated the cost of the trip. Choose the orders that suit you to get started.

5. Trip and payment

After completing each trips, it is necessary to complete the order through the app. This includes confirmation the end of the trip, the assessment of the passenger and the acceptance of payment from him. Uber provides various payment methods, including credit cards and e-wallets.

6. The Uber app has

functionality for tracking your income. You will be able to view statistics on orders, as well as information about earnings. This will help you control your finances and understand how improve your efficiency as a partner driver.

Driving experience requirement

For work as a partner driver with Uber, it is usually required to have more than 3 years of driving experience without serious incidents. This means that you should not have serious traffic violations, accidents involving you or reviews about your safety on the road in your driving past. Uber usually conducts checks of your driver's license and driving history to make sure that your suitability to work on their platform.
Make sure you meet the driving experience requirements before applying for work with Uber.
